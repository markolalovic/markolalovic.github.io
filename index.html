Welcome! I'm Marko. My research interests touch several areas of computer science, applied mathematics and statistics. Currently, my focus is on developing more effective algorithms with provable guarantees. I am also interested in developing applications of machine learning and designing AI systems.
I completed my MS degree in Industrial Mathematics at the University of Hamburg, Germany. My MS thesis was on Exact Algorithms for Dense Maximum Cut supervised by Prof Matthias Mnich at the Institute for Algorithms and Complexity, TUHH. Before that, I was studying for my MS in Applied Statistics and did my BS in Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of Ljubljana, Slovenia.
I worked on applications of machine learning in many fields including biotechnology, medicine and fraud prevention. My experience includes building, evaluating, and deploying large-scale models. Prior to that, I was a student researcher at two departments at Jozef Stefan Institute where I worked on applying deep learning to robotics and on performance evaluation of machine learning algorithms.
