My research interests lie at the intersection of theoretical computer science, applied mathematics, and machine learning. My current focus is on developing more effective algorithms with provable guarantees. I am also interested in developing applications, especially in areas such as bioinformatics and medical sciences. In particular when it comes to designing AI systems that are guided by structure and geometry.
I obtained a BSc in Computer Science and Mathematics from the University of Ljubljana, and a MSc in Industrial Mathematics from the University of Hamburg. I was a research intern at two departments at the Jozef Stefan Institute in Ljubljana and later worked at various tech startups, where I gained experience in building and deploying large-scale machine learning models.
