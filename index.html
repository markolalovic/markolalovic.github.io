My research interests touch several areas of computer science, applied mathematics and statistics. Currently, my focus is on developing more effective algorithms with provable guarantees. I am also interested in developing applications to computational science and designing AI systems. I enjoy applying mathematics to problems from various fields and employing techniques from machine learning and statistical analysis.
I obtained a BSc in Computer Science and Mathematics from the University of Ljubljana, and a MSc in Industrial Mathematics from the University of Hamburg. I was a research intern at two departments at the Jozef Stefan Institute in Ljubljana and worked at various tech startups, where I gained experience in building and deploying large-scale machine learning models.
